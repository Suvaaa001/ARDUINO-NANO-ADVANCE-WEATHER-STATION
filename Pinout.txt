🔌 Arduino Nano Pinout for Weather Station:

1️⃣ SH1106 OLED Display (I2C):
   - VCC  ->  5V
   - GND  ->  GND
   - SCL  ->  A5
   - SDA  ->  A4

2️⃣ DHT11 Temperature & Humidity Sensor:
   - VCC  ->  5V
   - GND  ->  GND
   - DATA ->  D2

3️⃣ MQ135 Air Quality Sensor:
   - VCC  ->  5V
   - GND  ->  GND
   - AOUT ->  A0
